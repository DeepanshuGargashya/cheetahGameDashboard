  • War of tribe:
    War of tribe is a team game. The stronger your team more chances of winning
    • How to Participate:- Any Cheetah NFT holder can participate in game. Cheetah can choose between Team A and Team B. Multiple entries allowed with different cheetah NFT. After participation players can get access to their respective team Chat Box
    • Wins:- Winning team will be decided on how much gas fee a particular NFT burned while training for Speedah
    • Game Dynamics:- Cheetah NFT’s Powers are auto upgraded based on the Gas fee which is dynamic after every match. The more you train the more stronger you will become.
    • Prize Distribution:- Prize will be distributed among the winning team. Prize for 1 Cheetah holder = Total Prize Pool / Total number of Cheetahs participated
  • Speedah Run: Speedah Run will be a Solo game to win more. Each cheetah will be consider one different entry. Multiple entries will not be allowed.
    • How to Participate:- Any Cheetah NFT holder can participate in game.Multiple entries allowed with different cheetah NFT.
    • Wins:- Winning team will be decided on how much gas fee a particular NFT burned while training for Speedah
    • Game Dynamics:- Cheetah NFT’s Powers are auto upgraded based on the Gas fee which is dynamic after every match. The more you train the more stronger you will become. One player will be selected based on power at the end of game.
    • Prize Distribution:- Prize will be distributed among the winning Cheetah. All the prizepool will be awarded to highest Power Cheetah.
