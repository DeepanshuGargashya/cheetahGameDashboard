About Us

5678 unimpressed Cheetahs roam the EVMOS ecosystem in search of grassland river streams to hydrate. Instead, they come across the city of Speedah, where sports and politics rule the masses.

Uncover unique identities, hidden secrets, and powerful traits by minting your own unimpressed Cheetah. Dominate the playing field and join our community on Discord and Twitter.

NFTs

NFTs, are certificates of ownership of a digital good made in limited numbers. For games this can be in-game items, collectibles, and avatars

Play to Earn

Frequently Asked Questions

Why Cheetah NFT ?

NFTs play a central role in how gamers play and earn, NFTs provide an extremely flexible primitive for P2E games to allow users to build their economies around. P2E games are globally available and instantly playable by anyone.

HOW CAN I GET WHITELISTED ? ?

We will hold competitions that users can participate in for the chance of winning a whitelist spot. If you win the competition, you will need to provide us with your Ethereum wallet address so that we can add it to the whitelist. Join our discord to know more and stay updated about events.

WHAT IS THE PRICE OF 1 CHEETAH ?

The price for 1 Cheetah will be 35 EVMOS in the Whitelist sale (presale). The price for the public sale will be 45 EVMOS per Cheetah.

What is Dashboard ?

Dashboard is a Cheetah`s profile , which provides analysis about game data, earnings, Cheetah Powers etc.

How to Play game?
